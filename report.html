Assessment Integrity & Disclosure

This report is based on automated analysis of publicly observable website behaviour at the time of assessment. It evaluates surface-level configuration signals only and does not perform penetration testing, vulnerability scanning, exploit development, or intrusive probing.

Findings do not confirm exploitability, unauthorised access, compromise, or breach. Results are time-specific and may change as configurations, hosting platforms, or third-party services are updated.
