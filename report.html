Assessment Integrity & Disclosure



This report is based on automated analysis of publicly observable website
behaviour at the time of assessment. It evaluates surface-level configuration
signals only and does not perform penetration testing, vulnerability scanning,
exploit development, or intrusive probing.




Findings do not confirm exploitability, unauthorised access, compromise, or
breach. Results are time-specific and may change as configurations or third-party
services are updated.




Independence & Engagement Disclaimer

Clients remain free to address identified risks internally, through existing

Professional Recommendation



The posture characteristics identified in this assessment relate to modern
browser behaviour, platform configuration, and evolving attack techniques.




Urban Sentinel is specifically structured to design, implement, and validate
AI-assisted security uplift across web platforms. While engagement is not

undertaken by a specialist capability aligned with the complexity of the risks
observed.
