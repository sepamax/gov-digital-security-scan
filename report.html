Observed Risk Implications



Missing or weak browser security controls may reduce protection against
client-side content injection, increase reliance on default browser
behaviour, and elevate exposure to third-party script risks over time.




Assessment Integrity & Disclosure



This report is based on automated analysis of publicly observable website
behaviour at the time of assessment. It evaluates surface-level
configuration indicators only and does not perform penetration testing,
active exploitation, or vulnerability scanning.




Findings do not confirm exploitability, compromise, or breach.
Results are time-specific and may change as configurations are updated.
